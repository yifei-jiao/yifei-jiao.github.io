---
title: Where should we impact an asteroid to effectively deflect it
date: 2026-01-01
summary: We introduce the concept of a Delta V hodograph when impacting a potentially hazardous asteroid considering the ejecta contribution. The optimal kinetic impact geometry is proposed to maximize the deflection distance given the orbital geometry. For a Bennu-like near spherical target, the optimal impact location could be off-centered and increase the effective deflection by up to 50%. For an Itokawa-like elongated target, the optimal impact direction could help improve the effective deflection by 100%.
