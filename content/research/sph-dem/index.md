---
title: SPH-DEM method modeling impacts on rubble-pile asteroids
date: 2024-01-01
summary: A new hybrid SPH–DEM framework is introduced to model the multistage dynamics in rubble-pile impacts, employing the surface contact particles and the interior gravity particles to describe large irregular boulders during long-term evolution. With the proposed method, the complexity of both the contact and gravity computation can be accelerated by ~100 times while maintaining the same level of resolution. The method is then used to simulate the DART impact on the rubble-pile asteroid Dimorphos.
tags: ["Asteroids", "Hybrid method"]
